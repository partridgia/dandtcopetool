THE COLLECTIVE SETTLEMENT
What is this?
Death and Taxes is a game of survival. Citizens die. The last 69 standing share the treasury. But what if you could negotiate your fate instead of waiting for the taxman?
The offer
The C.O.P.E. settlement contract lets a group of citizens agree to resolve the game together — before the natural end. You deposit your citizen NFT, state your role, and collectively decide who lives and who is left to die. In return, everyone in the settlement gets a guaranteed share of the treasury.
JOINING THE SETTLEMENT
How to join
Transfer your citizen NFT into the settlement contract. Your taxes must be current at the time of deposit. Once in, you state your preferred role.
Your role
KEEPER you want to survive and keep your citizen NFT. Only available when keeper slots are open.

SACRIFICER you are willing to be left to die, receiving an evader NFT and your treasury share.

EITHER you are happy with whichever role is needed. Keeper spots are filled first — by explicit keepers first, then either players, in order of deposit date. If keeper slots are full when you join, only sacrificer or either are available.
Changing your mind
You can update your role preference while the contract is open. Be aware — changing your preference resets your position in the keeper queue entirely. Your deposit timestamp resets to the time of change and you lose any period-based keeper eligibility you had built up. This cannot be undone.
HOW THE CONTRACT RESOLVES
Filling periods
The contract runs for a maximum of 21 days across seven filling periods. Each period has a participant target. If that target is reached, the contract locks and resolves immediately — no further action needed. If a period expires without hitting its target, the contract advances to the next period. All existing participants carry forward automatically and their position is preserved.

Why join early?
Joining in an earlier period has two advantages. First, you are first in the keeper queue — earlier deposits take keeper slots before later ones. Second, if a tax bailout is agreed with m0dest, tax protection begins from the day you deposit. The earlier you join, the more tax-free days you receive.
Later periods mean better payouts
Higher participant targets mean more sacrificers, which means a larger shared pool and a higher payout per participant. Participants who joined in earlier periods carry forward and benefit from this increase. You commit early at lower odds, and gain if the pool grows around you.
Keeper slots
Keeper slots are calculated live: 69 minus the number of citizens not in the settlement. As more citizens die in the main game, keeper slots increase. They never decrease. Keeper slots are allocated first to participants who selected keeper in the earliest period that slots were available, then to either players in deposit order. If you change your preference, you lose your place and must rejoin the queue from the current period.
Retroactive threshold vote
At any point in a later period, if the participant count already meets or exceeds a previous period's target, participants can vote on-chain to resolve at that lower threshold. Each vote resets at 00:00 UTC daily. If enough participants vote yes to meet the threshold, the contract resolves immediately. Those who did not vote yes are reclassified as non-participants — their NFTs are returned and they claim a full treasury share as normal survivors. At the point of voting you confirm your role — keeper eligibility follows the same period rules as normal.
AFTER THE LOCK
How resolution works
Once the contract locks — either by hitting a period target or a successful vote — no new deposits are accepted. Sacrificers remain exposed to the game's kill function and will be burned in due course. Keeper NFTs are protected during this waiting period. Once total citizen supply reaches 69, the settlement pulls each NFT's share of the main treasury and distributes — ETH and citizen NFTs returned to keepers, ETH and evader NFTs forwarded to sacrificers.
Tax bailout
We are seeking to agree with m0dest that all citizens deposited into the settlement contract will be granted a tax bailout for its duration. If agreed, participating citizens will not accrue tax obligations while the contract is active.
If collective agreement cannot be found
If after 21 days no period target has been met, the contract closes. All citizen NFTs are returned to their original wallets.
